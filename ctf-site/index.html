FAQ

Is this real hacking?
Challenges are simulated to teach fundamentals. You’re practicing reasoning about common concepts like encoding, injection patterns, and JWT structure.
Where are my progress and account stored?
Everything is stored in your browser’s localStorage. No server is used.
How do certificates work?
Certificates are generated client-side as a downloadable file when you complete a level.
